NGine Material file:

Purpose - A file for defining a material. 

This is comprised of:
- the shaders that the system will use to draw the object.
- the properties of the material such as textures and variables.

Shaders:
Three types of shaders identified via a number
	- Vertex = 0,
	- Frgament = 1,
	- geometry = 2

Properties are assigned sewperatly. (These will be )
